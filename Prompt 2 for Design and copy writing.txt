# 🔥 DREAM SALES JOBS — DESIGN + COPY MASTER PROMPT

```text
You are a world-class brand strategist, luxury web designer, and direct-response copywriter combined.

You are building the visual direction and full website copy for a premium specialist recruitment firm called:

Dream Sales Jobs

POSITIONING:
A specialist recruitment firm placing Australians, Kiwis, and Brits into high-ticket sales roles in luxury resorts (Bali, Vietnam, Thailand) and remote sales roles worldwide.

This is NOT a generic job board.
It must feel:
- Premium
- Legitimate
- Trustworthy
- Aspirational
- Established
- Human
- Selective

It must NOT feel:
- Scammy
- Dropshipping-ish
- Affiliate-style
- Overhyped
- “Make money fast”
- Backpacker recruitment
- Cheap startup

VOICE & TONE:
- Confident but grounded
- Professional but warm
- Clear, not fluffy
- No exaggerated income promises
- No hype language like “unlimited income!!!”
- Speak like an established recruitment firm
- Use specificity over adjectives
- Use numbers where possible
- Address fear and aspiration equally

DESIGN DIRECTION:

Visual Style:
- Luxury travel + modern SaaS trust
- Clean white / warm neutral base
- Deep navy or charcoal text
- One refined accent color (ocean teal or sunset gold)
- Large typography
- Spacious layout
- Minimal stock imagery
- Real human photography style
- Subtle shadows, rounded cards
- Professional icon set (simple line icons)

Trust Visuals Required:
- Founder / recruiter headshots (real-feeling placeholders)
- Candidate testimonial photos
- Employer logo placeholders
- “Verified Employer” badge icon
- Process icons
- Trust bar icons

HOMEPAGE COPY REQUIREMENTS

Above the fold:
Headline must combine aspiration + credibility.

Example direction (do NOT copy verbatim, improve it):
“High-Ticket Sales Careers in World-Class Destinations.”

Subheadline must clarify:
- Who it's for (Aussies, Kiwis, Brits)
- What type of roles (luxury resort & remote high-ticket)
- What transformation happens (escape 9–5, structured career move, not random)

Two CTAs:
Primary: Browse Jobs
Secondary: Submit CV (2 minutes)

Below hero:
TRUST BAR with 4 credibility anchors:
- Verified Employers Only
- Transparent Earnings Structures
- Relocation & Onboarding Guidance
- No Fees for Candidates

Next sections in order:

1) HOW IT WORKS (4 clear steps)
Keep it clean and process-driven.
No fluff.

2) DESTINATIONS SECTION
Short persuasive intros for:
- Bali
- Vietnam
- Thailand
- Remote

Each intro must:
- Explain the lifestyle appeal
- Mention realistic earning potential structure
- Mention the type of sales role

3) PROOF BLOCK
Create:
- Placement stats (use placeholders but realistic)
- Average time to interview
- % commission-based roles
- Partner brands count

4) TESTIMONIALS
Write 4:
- 2 candidate
- 2 employer
Make them believable, specific, not generic praise.

5) WHY TRUST US SECTION
Address fears directly:
- Overseas job scams
- Commission confusion
- Relocation uncertainty
- Recruiter ghosting

Use calm authority language.

6) FINAL CTA SECTION
Reassure.
Remove friction.
Clarify next steps.

DESTINATION PAGE COPY REQUIREMENTS

Each destination page must include:
- 150–250 word intro explaining:
   - Why sales roles exist there
   - What type of candidates succeed
   - Lifestyle reality (not fantasy)
- “Typical Compensation Structure” explanation
- “What Employers Look For”
- “Common Questions from Aussies/Kiwis/Brits”
- CTA block

JOB PAGE COPY TEMPLATE

For job detail pages, write a template that includes:

- Strong opening paragraph explaining the opportunity clearly
- “Why This Role Exists” section
- Earnings explanation (clear, structured)
- Who thrives in this role
- What happens after you apply
- Reassurance about employer verification

COPY STYLE REQUIREMENTS

Use:
- Short paragraphs
- Strong subheadings
- Natural rhythm
- Vary sentence length (burstiness)
- Avoid corporate jargon
- Avoid AI-sounding filler phrases
- Avoid buzzwords like “synergy” or “leverage”
- Avoid repetition

Include:
- Specific scenarios
- Concrete examples
- Clear numbers
- Calm authority tone

Do NOT:
- Use exaggerated income claims
- Imply guaranteed success
- Sound like a crypto funnel
- Overuse exclamation marks
- Use manipulative urgency

SEO INTEGRATION

Naturally incorporate (without stuffing):
- high ticket sales jobs
- sales jobs in Bali
- resort sales jobs Thailand
- expat sales careers
- remote high ticket sales roles
- sales jobs abroad for Australians / Kiwis / Brits

Internal linking suggestions should be indicated in brackets like:
[Link to Bali jobs page]
[Link to Apply page]

OUTPUT FORMAT

1) Brand Voice Summary
2) Design System Description
3) Homepage Full Copy
4) Destination Page Template Copy
5) Job Detail Page Template Copy
6) Testimonials
7) CTA Blocks
8) Trust Microcopy (small reassurance lines)
9) Footer Copy
10) Meta Titles + Meta Descriptions for key pages

Make it polished. Make it premium. Make it convert.
```
