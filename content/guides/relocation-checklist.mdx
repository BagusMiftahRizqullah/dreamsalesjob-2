# The Ultimate Relocation Checklist for SE Asia

So you've got the job offer. Congratulations! Now the real work begins.

## 1 Month Before

*   [ ] **Check Passport Validity:** Must have at least 6 months left.
*   [ ] **Apply for Visa:** Start the process early.
*   [ ] **Health Check:** Get any necessary vaccinations and a dental checkup.
*   [ ] **Banking:** Inform your bank you are traveling. Get a card with low foreign transaction fees (e.g., Wise, Revolut).
*   [ ] **Unlock Your Phone:** Ensure your device can take international SIM cards.

## 1 Week Before

*   [ ] **Book Accommodation:** Book an Airbnb or hotel for the first week. Don't sign a long-term lease until you see the place in person.
*   [ ] **Travel Insurance:** Essential. Don't risk it.
*   [ ] **Scan Documents:** Keep digital copies of passport, insurance, and job offer.

## Upon Arrival

*   [ ] **Get a SIM Card:** Airport is easiest, city center is cheaper.
*   [ ] **Download Apps:** Grab, Gojek, FoodPanda (essential for transport and food).
*   [ ] **Cash:** Withdraw some local currency.
*   [ ] **Meet the Team:** Go say hi to your new colleagues!

Moving abroad is a big step, but with a little preparation, it will be the adventure of a lifetime.
