# 5 Tips for Crushing It in Remote Sales

Working from paradise sounds dreamy, but it comes with distractions. Here is how top performers stay focused.

## 1. Dedicated Workspace

Don't work from your bed. Find a co-working space or set up a proper desk in your apartment. You need a mental trigger that says "it's work time."

## 2. Time Zone Management

If you're selling to the US or UK from Asia, you'll be working odd hours.
*   **US Market:** Late nights (9 PM - 5 AM).
*   **UK Market:** Afternoons/Evenings (3 PM - 11 PM).
*   **AU Market:** Early mornings (6 AM - 2 PM).

Embrace the schedule. Use your free time during the day to surf, gym, or relax.

## 3. Reliable Internet is Non-Negotiable

Get a backup connection. Mobile data in SE Asia is cheap and fast. Always have a hotspot ready for when the WiFi drops.

## 4. Over-Communicate

In a remote team, silence is dangerous. Update your manager frequently. Slack is your office. Be visible.

## 5. Find Your Tribe

Surround yourself with other high achievers. It's easy to fall into "holiday mode" if all your friends are backpackers. Hang out with other professionals who are grinding.
