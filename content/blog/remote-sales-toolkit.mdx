Closing deals from a Bali villa or a Thai beach sounds idyllic, but you need the right setup.

## Hardware

*   **MacBook Air/Pro**: Reliable, long battery.
*   **Noise-cancelling Headset**: Jabra Evolve 75 or Bose QC45.
*   **Portable Monitor**: ASUS ZenScreen.

## Software

*   **Zoom/Google Meet**: Stable video calls.
*   **CRM**: HubSpot or Salesforce.
*   **Loom**: Asynchronous video for follow-ups.
*   **Calendly**: Scheduling made easy.

## Internet

Always have a backup 4G/5G hotspot. Starlink is becoming popular for remote areas.
