Is the London fog and high tax burden getting to you? Moving to Bali or Thailand is easier than you think.

## Tax Benefits

While the UK has up to 45% income tax, Southeast Asian countries have favorable tax regimes for expats, often capping at 15-20% or even 0% for remote income (territorial tax).

## Lifestyle Upgrade

Trade the Tube for a scooter, Pret sandwiches for Pad Thai, and gloomy weekends for island hopping.

1.  **3 Months Out**: Apply for jobs.
2.  **2 Months Out**: Secure offer & visa.
3.  **1 Month Out**: Book flights, give notice.
4.  **Launch**: Arrive and start your new life.
