Thailand is actively courting high-income earners with new visa categories.

## LTR Visa (Long-Term Resident)

This is the golden ticket. 10-year visa, reduced tax rates (17% flat), and no 90-day reporting.

### Eligibility

*   $80k USD annual income over past 2 years
*   Or $40k USD with a Master's degree or intellectual property
*   Or work for a public company or one with >$150M revenue

## Smart Visa

Designed for tech talents and investors in targeted industries. Perfect for SaaS sales roles in BOI-promoted companies.

## Standard Business Visa (Non-B) + Work Permit

The traditional route. Your employer must sponsor this.
