## Visa, Skills, and Money Real Talk

No shortcuts on paperwork: Aussies need a KITAS (now ~11.25M IDR post-2024 hikes) sponsored by your employer—Dream Sales Jobs guides clients through this for seamless starts. Grab TEFL for teaching backups or sales certs for vacation roles.[](https://flado.id/2024/12/18/visa-price-changes-in-bali-who-will-pay-more-to-stay-in-indonesia/)​

Skills that pop: Proven closing (we prioritize this), English fluency, cultural flex. Budget wise? Comfortable expat life runs IDR 15-25M/month ($1,000-1,700 USD)—villa rent IDR 8M, meals IDR 3M, scooter IDR 1M. Stash 3 months’ savings; health insurance is non-negotiable.[](https://ilotpropertybali.com/cost-of-living-in-bali/)​

## Settling In Like a Local

Score a Canggu homestay first (IDR 5M/month), then upgrade to a pool villa. Hit BIMC Hospital for checkups—private plans cover evac too. Build your crew via sales meetups (we host ’em!) or surf sessions; learning “terima kasih” opens doors fast.
