Bali isn't just for digital nomads and influencers anymore. It's becoming a hub for high-ticket sales professionals.

## Why Bali?

Imagine closing deals by the pool in a luxury villa. The cost of living is low, and the quality of life is incredibly high.

### Types of Roles available

1. **Real Estate**: Selling villas to investors.
2. **Timeshare**: High-energy resort sales.
3. **Remote Tech**: Selling SaaS to US/EU markets.

## Visa Requirements

Most sales professionals start on a B211A Business Visa and then transition to a KITAS (Work Permit) once sponsored by their employer.

## Earning Potential

Senior sales roles in Bali can command OTEs of $100k - $200k USD per year, tax-efficiently.
