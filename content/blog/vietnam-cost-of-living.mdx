Vietnam is arguably the best value-for-money destination in Southeast Asia right now.

## Monthly Budget Breakdown (Single Expat)

| Item | Cost (USD) | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Housing** | $500 - $800 | Modern 1-bedroom apartment with gym/pool |
| **Food** | $300 | Eating out for almost every meal |
| **Transport** | $50 | Grab bikes and taxis |
| **Utilities** | $50 | Electricity, water, internet |
| **Entertainment** | $200 | Drinks, movies, weekend trips |
| **Total** | **$1,100 - $1,400** | |

## Salary Expectations

Sales roles in Vietnam often pay base salaries of $1,500 - $3,000 USD + uncapped commissions.

It is very realistic to save **$2,000+ per month** while living a very comfortable lifestyle.

## Best Cities

*   **Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon):** The economic engine. Fast-paced, chaotic, exciting. Best for career growth.
*   **Da Nang:** The chill coastal city. Beach vibes, less traffic, digital nomad hub.
*   **Hanoi:** The cultural capital. More traditional, distinct seasons.

Vietnam offers an incredible opportunity to stack cash while enjoying a rich cultural experience.
